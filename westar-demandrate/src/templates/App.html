What is "peak demand"?

Demand is the rate at which you consume electricity â€“ or that amount needed to power your home or business at any given point in time. Peak Demand is the amount of energy your home or business needed during the highest 15-minute interval of this billing period. Using all of your devices at one time will max out your peak demand, so by planning tasks and using less devices at one time, your demand can be significantly lower.

Avoid running high-energy appliances together to keep your peak demand low. Click the icons to see how shutting down some of your appliances can lower your energy costs!
